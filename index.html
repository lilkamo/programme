Programme rules
Soviet hybrid system. Climb to a top set, drop back for volume. Best of both worlds for strength and fat loss simultaneously.
Deload weeks 4, 8, 12. Eat at maintenance. Non-negotiable — adaptation happens here.
Protein every day. 170–200g daily. Missing this is the main cause of strength stalls in a deficit.
Sleep 7–9 hours. Under 7 raises cortisol and ghrelin — both stall fat loss even in a deficit.
3.5L water daily. At 127kg training 6 days a week, hydration directly impacts performance and recovery.
Never skip deloads. Weeks 4, 8, 12 are mandatory rest weeks. Skipping them stalls progress.
Cardio placement matters. Bike on squat days, incline walk on others. Never high intensity the day before deadlifts.
