Programme rules
Soviet hybrid system. Climb to a top set then drop back for volume. Best of both worlds for strength and fat loss simultaneously.
Deload weeks 4, 8, 12. Eat at maintenance. Non-negotiable — this is when adaptation happens.
170–200g protein daily. The main reason strength stalls in a deficit is missing this.
Friday squats stay light. You deadlift Saturday. Friday is technique, not intensity.
3.5L water daily. At 127kg training 6 days a week hydration directly impacts performance.
